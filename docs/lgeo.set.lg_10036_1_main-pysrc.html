110  '''*************************************************************************** 
111  LGEO Disclaimer 
112  Well, it is possible but not recommended. LGEO uses a different coordinate 
113  system and scaling than LDRAW, so it may be pretty hard to build a model by 
114  placing the parts by hand. In LGEO, for historic reasons Y axis is the depth 
115  axis, and Z is the up axis, where also Z has switched sign to LDRAW Y axis. 
116  Sorry for that, but my understanding of coordinate to this time was a flat 
117  standard XY coordinate system with a third Z axis coming out of the plane. 
118  Scaling is to real measurements, where 1 POV-Ray unit is 10mm. So 0.8 is 20 
119  LDU (width of a LEGO brick) and 0.96 is 24 LDU (height of a LEGO brick). 
120  ***************************************************************************'''
